# Legal Citation Validation

## Case Law -- Neutral Citations (post-2001)
Format: [YEAR] COURT NUMBER (Division)
- High Court: [2024] EWHC 1234 (Ch)/(QB)/(Admin)/(Comm)/(Fam)/(KB)
- Court of Appeal: [2024] EWCA Civ 1234 / Crim 1234
- Supreme Court: [2024] UKSC 1234

Year in square brackets, not round. Division in parentheses after number.

## Statute Citations
- Section: s.50 (not Section 50 or S.50)
- Subsection: s.50(1)(a)
- Act: Administration of Justice Act 1985

## CPR References
- Rules: CPR r.25.1(1)(c)(v) -- lowercase r.
- Parts: CPR Part 25
- Practice Directions: PD 32 para 18.1

## Exhibit Cross-References
- Format: [SP1] -- consistent throughout
- Reference MUST appear before exhibit in bundle
- Never show page numbers in display text
- Every reference must have corresponding exhibit
- Every exhibit must be referenced

## Common Errors
1. Wrong brackets: (2024) instead of [2024]
2. Missing neutral citation
3. Mixed exhibit formats (SP1 vs Exhibit 1)
4. Orphan exhibits / phantom references
5. "Section 50" instead of "s.50"
6. Wrong court abbreviation (EWCH vs EWHC)
